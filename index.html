Who I am

My name is Carlos Esquer, a web designer & developer based out of sunny San Diego, Ca.

In 2007 I moved to San Diego to pursue a career path as a web designer. I attended the Art Institute of California-San Diego and earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Web Design & Interactive Media. As of 2011, I began working at great up and coming tech company named Underground Elephant. I recently began freelancing and starting to promote my brand.

What I do

I enjoy putting ideas together and translating them into a digital experience that produces results.

As a child I was always creative and enjoyed designing. During summer breaks in high school I was glued to my computer learning how to build websites and exploring the possiblitieswith coding. Once I was was introduced to Photoshop and its possibilities, I began realizing being a web designer was definately a career option I wanted to pursue.
